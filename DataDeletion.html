1) Important: Sign out is not deletion

Using Sign out only ends your current session. It does not delete your Payback account or the records you created in the app.

2) How to request deletion (entry point: “Contact Us”)

Payback handles account/data deletion requests through the in-app support channel.

Steps

  1. Open Payback
  2. Tap Contact Us
  3. In your message, write: “Data Deletion Request” (or “Delete Account Request”)
  4. Submit the request

Please include (to help us locate and verify your account):

  • the sign-in method you used (Facebook / Google / Apple),
  • your in-app nickname (if any),
  • (optional) the email address shown in your sign-in provider account.

Verification
 To protect your account, we may ask for additional information to verify you are the account owner before we complete deletion.

3) Service level / processing time (SLA)

Acknowledgement: We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within 5 business days.
Completion: After verification, we aim to complete deletion within 30 calendar days.

If your request is complex (for example, it involves shared records that must be handled carefully) we may take longer, but we will update you through the same support channel.

4) What will be deleted

After your request is verified and processed, we will delete (or irreversibly de-identify where appropriate) personal data associated with your Payback account, including:

  • your Payback account identifier(s),
  • your expense records, splits, bill reminders, income logs (tracking), notes/descriptions,
  • your saved PayMe link and/or uploaded PayMe/Alipay QR code (if any),
  • your in-app friend connections and related sharing permissions.

Shared splits (how deletion works)

Where records are shared with other users (for example, an expense split involving multiple people), we will handle deletion in a way that protects your personal data while maintaining the integrity of other users’ records. This may involve:

  • removing your payment link/QR code,
  • removing or anonymising your profile/nickname in shared records (e.g., showing “Deleted user”),
  • deleting your private notes/content that are not required for other users’ bookkeeping.

5) What we may retain (limited)

We may retain limited information where required or permitted by law, or for legitimate purposes such as:

  • security and fraud/abuse prevention,
  • maintaining system integrity and reliability (e.g., server logs),
  • handling disputes or enforcing our Terms.

Where we retain information, we limit access and keep it only for as long as necessary for the retention purpose.

6) If you used Facebook Login (Meta)

If you signed in using Facebook Login, you can also remove Payback’s access from your Facebook settings. Removing access helps stop future data sharing from Facebook to Payback, but it does not automatically delete your Payback account data. To delete your Payback data, please submit a deletion request via Contact Us as described above.

7) Contact

All deletion requests must be submitted via the in-app Contact Us entry point so we can verify account ownership and communicate updates.
