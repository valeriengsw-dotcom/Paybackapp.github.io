1. What Payback is (and is not)

Payback helps you record and organize:

  • shared expense splits,
  • bill payment records and reminders, and
  • income records (for tracking only).

Payback is not a bank, payment service, wallet, accounting service, or debt collection service. We do not process payments, hold money, or guarantee that anyone will pay you.

2. Sign-in and login providers

Payback uses third-party sign-in (such as Google, Facebook, or Apple ID) to authenticate users.

  • We do not store your third-party password.
  • Your use of those providers is subject to their terms and policies.

3. Your content and responsibility for records

All records (expenses, splits, bill reminders, income logs, notes) are based on what users enter.

  • You are responsible for checking amounts, confirming what is owed, and confirming that payment has actually been made.
  • Reminders and notifications are provided for convenience only. We cannot guarantee a reminder will always be delivered on time or at all (for example, due to device settings, network issues, or operating system restrictions).

4. Payment links and QR codes (PayMe / Alipay)

Payback may allow you to paste/enter a PayMe link and/or upload a PayMe or Alipay QR code to help settle expenses between friends.

Important:

  • Payback does not verify payment links/QR codes and is not responsible for payments made outside the app.
  • Tapping a PayMe link is intended to route you to the PayMe app (if available).

5. Friends, splitting, and limited sharing

  • Friend connections require mutual acceptance.
  • Your payment link/QR code is visible only to the splitter in an expense split where you are involved, as described in the Privacy Policy.
